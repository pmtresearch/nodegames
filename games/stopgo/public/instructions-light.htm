Instructions Summary

Game starts in seconds.
  • You are about to be assigned a color: Red or Blue.
  • You will play 3 matches, each one with a different player of the other color.
  • Red player goes first, knows the state of the world, and chooses STOP or GO.
  • Blue player follows, does not know the state of the world, and chooses LEFT or RIGHT.
  • Probability of the state of the world being A is __ .
  • How your choices affect your payoff will be shown for each decision you will take.
  • The history of past matches is available after the first match.
