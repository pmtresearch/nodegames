Instructions of the Trust Game. Please read them carefully

This game is played in rounds by two human players randomly paired.

In each round, one of the them, called TRUSTEE, makes an offer to the other player, called TRUSTOR, about how to share 100 ECU (Experimental Currency). 100 ECU are equal to 0.05 USD.

The TRUSTOR can either accept or reject the offer of the TRUSTEE. If he / she accepts, both players split 100 ECU accordingly, else both get 0.

The game is repeated 20 rounds, therefore the maximum bonus available is 1 dollar.

If you understood the instructions correctly press the button to proceed to the game.
